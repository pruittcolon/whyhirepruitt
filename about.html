About Me

AI Engineer • Full-Stack Developer • IoT Systems Architect

My Journey: Telecom to Software Engineering to AI

I began in fiber optics and telecom, where I earned my Lean Six Sigma Green Belt and learned to think in systems. Lean methodology taught me to identify bottlenecks, eliminate waste, and optimize complex workflows - skills that became the foundation of how I approach software architecture. Watching senior engineers solve impossible network issues sparked my transition into software engineering - I wanted to build the tools that solve those problems at scale.

That spark led me to earn a Master's in Software Engineering (AI Specialization) from Western Governors University and a Bachelor's in Computer Science. I apply lean systems thinking to software development - breaking down complex AI systems into efficient, scalable components with minimal coupling and maximum value delivery.

Today, I architect AI platforms - most notably NeMo AI Ecosystem, a complete voice-controlled life automation system with 12,000+ lines of Python code. It integrates AR wearables (Even Reality glasses), IoT hubs, smart home devices, and enterprise AI (NeMo ASR, Gemma LLM, RAG) into a unified platform. I specialize in PyTorch, FastAPI, Docker, IoT orchestration, and CUDA optimization for real-world deployment.
